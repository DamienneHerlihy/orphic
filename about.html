What Is It?

Orphic allows members once registered to make a one-time purchase of our
Dream Memory Chip that records dreams as you sleep. Your dreams are
summarised down by us to tell you what they mean in terms of your future.
This could be health, work, love, and more..

How It Works?

The chip is injected in behind the ear where it remotely sends your dreams as
you sleep to our team of dream analysts. Your dreams are then decoded and
their meanings are posted to your profile where you can take the knowledge
learned to change your future. However, before you can start taking advantage
of our advanced dream technology you must make an appointment with your
local Orphic center to get your chip injected, go to our Contact page to do this.
